WLAN NameXXB-MSR_01 Change WLAN name

Service type:Manage WLAN

Service status
Hide SSID

Hiding SSID disables users from discovering a WLAN to improve WLAN security.
To discover the WLAN, enter the SSID on the client manually.

Encryption Service

Encryption service enables client traffic to be encrypted in the WLAN to improve WLAN security.

For WLANs using WeChat WiFi and other commercial functions, do not use the encryption service as a best practice.
